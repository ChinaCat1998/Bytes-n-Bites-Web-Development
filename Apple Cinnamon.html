Apple Cinnamon Walnut Coffee Cake

Ingredients

    Instructions


    • Heat oven to 375Â°F.
    • Grease or spray with non-stick spray a 13 x 9-inch pan.
    • Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in bowl. Stir in oil, egg, milk and apples. Mix well. Put in pan.

    Topping:

    • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    • 2 tbsp. flour
    • 2 tbsp. cinnamon
    • 6 tbsp. butter
    • 1 cup chopped walnuts

    Instructions


    • Mix together brown sugar and cinnamon all in bowl.
    • Sprinkle over batter. Drizzle butter over topping. Scatter on walnuts. (Can be done ahead and refrigerated at this part. Room temperature before baking).
    • Bake until toothpick inserted comes out clean, about 35 minutes. Cool.
