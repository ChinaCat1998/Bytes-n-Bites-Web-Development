Apple Cinnamon Walnut Coffee Cake

CAUTION: CONTAINS NUTS, MILK, AND EGGS

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 chopped apples

Instructions


  • Heat oven to 375Â°F.
  • Grease or spray with non-stick spray a 13 x 9-inch pan.
  • Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in bowl. Stir in oil, egg, milk and apples. Mix well. Put in pan.

Topping:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp. flour
  • 2 tbsp. cinnamon
  • 6 tbsp. butter
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions


  • Mix together brown sugar and cinnamon all in bowl.
  • Sprinkle over batter. Drizzle butter over topping. Scatter on walnuts. (Can be done ahead and refrigerated at this part. Room temperature before baking).
  • Bake until toothpick inserted comes out clean, about 35 minutes. Cool.

Glaze:

  • 1/3 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp. milk

Instructions


  • While cake is still warm, drizzle glaze over top.
  • To make glaze, mix confectioners' sugar, vanilla and milk until smooth in a bowl; if glaze is too thin, adjust consistency by adding more sugar.
  • Add water and stir if glaze is too thick.
  • While cake is still warm, drizzle glaze over top. To make glaze, mix confectioners' sugar, vanilla and milk until smooth in a bowl; if glaze is too thin, adjust consistency by adding more sugar.
  • Add water and stir if glaze is too thick.
  • Enjoy!
