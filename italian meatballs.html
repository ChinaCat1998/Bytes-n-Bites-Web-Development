Italian Sausage Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb sweet Italian sausage, bulk
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  • Crumble sausage, combine with beef, garlic, parsley, egg, bread crumbs, salt and pepper in large bowl.
  • Pour milk over. Mix thoroughly.
  • Form meatballs and brown in oil.
  • Enjoy!
