Salmon Fillets

CAUTION: CONTAINS FISH

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 4 salmon fillets (fresh from market)
  • 1 tbsp. butter, melted
  • 4 thin slices lemon
  • 8 tsp. brown sugar

Instructions

  • Heat oven to 375°F.
  • Pour lemon juice into ungreased rectangular baking dish, 11x7x2-inch; sprinkle with 1/4 cup brown sugar. Arrange fillets in dish; drizzle with butter. Bake uncovered 15 minutes; turn. Place 1 slice lemon on each salmon fillet; sprinkle with 2 teaspoons brown sugar

  • Enjoy!
