Mango Smoothie

CAUTION: CONTAINS DAIRY

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 mango, peeled
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • a few drops of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • honey or agave syrup, to taste

Instructions

  • Freeze the banana, the mango, and the yogurt for 1 hour before making the smoothie.
  • When ready, combine all ingredients in blender and process until smooth. Serve cold.
  • Enjoy!
